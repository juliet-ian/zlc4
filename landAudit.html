Agricultural Land Audit

The Agricultural Land Audit is a continuous process of evaluating and reviewing of all agricultural land. It involves gathering of all data relating to the land parcel, land owner, land occupant/user, land rights, land use, land usage, land management and infrastducture/improvements that exist on the agricultural land. A Land Audit is carried out once in every five years.

Objectives of Conducting Land Audit

The overall objective of the National Agricultural Land Audit is to establish a full account of the utilization of all agricultural land in Zimbabwe for providing economic growth, social equity and environmental sustainability.
